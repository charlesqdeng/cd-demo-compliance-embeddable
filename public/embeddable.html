Toll Free Number Verification

Submit your verification documents securely using Twilio's official compliance portal.

What You'll Need

  • Toll-free phone number (e.g., +18001234567)
  • Business name, type, and registration details
  • Business website with https:// (e.g., https://example.com)
  • Physical business address
  • Privacy Policy URL with https:// (e.g., https://example.com/privacy)
  • Terms of Service URL with https:// (e.g., https://example.com/terms)
  • Contact information (name, email, phone)
  • Messaging use case description
  • Sample message content (standard, HELP, STOP)
  • Opt-in workflow details
  • Expected monthly message volume
⚠️ Important: All URLs must include https:// or http:// prefix.

Enter Toll-Free Number
