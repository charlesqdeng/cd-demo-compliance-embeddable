Toll Free Number Verification

Submit your verification documents securely and get your toll-free number approved quickly.

📋 What You'll Need

Click the ℹ icons next to form fields for detailed guidance, examples, and tips.

  • Toll-free number - Must be purchased in your Twilio account
  • Business information - Legal name, type, registration number
  • Physical address - No P.O. Boxes
  • Website URLs - Business site, privacy policy, terms (must include https://)
  • Contact details - Business email and phone
  • Use case - Detailed description of how you'll use messaging
  • Message samples - Realistic examples including STOP language
  • Opt-in process - How customers consent to messages
  • Screenshots - Images showing opt-in workflow (if applicable)
💡 Pro Tip: Be specific and detailed in your descriptions. Vague answers lead to rejection.
⚠️ Common Mistakes:
  • Using P.O. Box instead of physical address
  • Forgetting https:// in URLs
  • Vague use case descriptions
  • No STOP language in message samples
✅ Review Time: 1-2 business days
